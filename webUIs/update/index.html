Health Centralized

Oftentimes the most impactful data points are the least measured. Your health is important and requires attention. If you have an iPhone and/or apple watch you are collecting value health data. How much of it are you activily using?

This app will upload & centralize your data to our cloud infrastructure where analytics and charts will visualize your progress toward your individualized health goals.
